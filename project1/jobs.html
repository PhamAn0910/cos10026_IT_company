Software Engineer

Job Reference Number: SE123

Position Title: Software Engineer

Salary Range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year

Reports to: Senior Software Engineer

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Software Engineer to develop, maintain, and optimize high-performance software applications.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop, test, and deploy software solutions.
  2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design applications.
  3. Write clean, maintainable, and scalable code.
  4. Perform code reviews and optimize software for efficiency.

Qualifications

Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • At least 3 years of experience in software development.
  • Proficiency in Python, Java, or C++.
  • Strong understanding of algorithms and data structures.

Preferable

  • Experience with cloud computing platforms (AWS, Azure).
  • Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Job Reference Number: CY987

Position Title: Cybersecurity Analyst

Salary Range: $90,000 - $110,000 per year

Reports to: Chief Security Officer

Job Description

We are seeking a Cybersecurity Analyst to protect our IT systems and infrastructure against cyber threats and vulnerabilities.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Monitor and analyze security threats.
  2. Implement security policies and best practices.
  3. Perform vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
  4. Investigate security incidents and respond accordingly.

Qualifications

Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in cybersecurity.
  • Knowledge of firewalls, encryption, and authentication protocols.
  • Experience with SIEM tools.

Preferable

  • Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) or CISSP certification.
  • Experience with forensic analysis and threat intelligence.
