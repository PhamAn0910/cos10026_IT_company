Data Analyst

Job Reference Number: DA123

Position Title: Data Analyst

Salary Range: $80,000 - $110,000 per annum

Reports to: Head of Data Science

Job Description

SonixWave is seeking a skilled Data Analyst to collect, analyze, and interpret user listening patterns to enhance personalized music recommendations. This role plays a crucial part in increasing user engagement by optimizing song and genre suggestions.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Analyze large datasets of user listening behaviors to identify trends and patterns.
  2. Develop and refine algorithms for personalized music recommendations.
  3. Collaborate with software engineers and product managers to integrate data-driven insights into the platform.
  4. Conduct A/B testing to measure the effectiveness of recommendation models.
  5. Prepare and present data-driven reports to stakeholders.
  6. Ensure data integrity and compliance with privacy regulations.

Qualifications

Essential

  • Bachelor' degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in SQL and Python/R for data analysis.
  • Strong experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib).
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in data analytics, preferably in a music or media tech company.
  • Understanding of machine learning techniques related to recommendation systems.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Preferable

  • Masters' degree in a related field.
  • Experience with big data technologies such as Hadoop, Spark, or Google BigQuery.
  • Familiarity with AI-driven personalization techniques.
  • Passion for music and knowledge of different genres.

Software Engineer (Backend)

Job Reference Number: CY987

Position Title: Software Engineer (Backend)

Salary Range: $90,000 - $130,000 per annum

Reports to: Engineering Manager

Job Description

SonixWave is looking for a talented Backend Software Engineer to develop and maintain the core infrastructure that powers our music recommendation and streaming platform. This role focuses on ensuring seamless data processing and API integrations to provide a smooth user experience.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend systems to support high-volume music streaming.
  2. Build and optimize APIs for real-time music recommendations and user interactions.
  3. Work closely with data scientists to deploy machine learning models in production.
  4. Ensure database efficiency, security, and scalability.
  5. Collaborate with frontend developers to enhance platform responsiveness.
  6. Troubleshoot and debug backend issues to maintain high system reliability.

Qualifications

Essential

  • Bachelor' degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in backend programming languages such as Python, Java, or Node.js.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in backend development, preferably in a media streaming company.
  • Strong knowledge of RESTful API design and database management (SQL & NoSQL).
  • Familiarity with microservices architecture and distributed systems.

Preferable

  • Master's degree in a related field.
  • Experience with containerization tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Understanding of music streaming protocols and audio processing technologies.
  • Passion for music technology and innovation.
